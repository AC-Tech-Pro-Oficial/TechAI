AC Tech Solutions (AC Tech) - Comprehensive Company Analysis

## 1. Corporate Identity and Core Mission

**Official Name:** AC Tech Solutions (AC Tech)
**Founders:** Moacir Bilhar Costa Junior & Vinicyus Abdala
**Ownership Structure:** 50/50 Equal Partnership
**Headquarters:** Brazil (Operating under Brazilian Legislation)
**Primary Domain:** ac-tech.pro

**Mission Statement:**
AC Tech aims to revolutionize traditional service industries and educational sectors through high-end technological solutions. The company positions itself not merely as a software house but as a provider of "Technological Solutions," encompassing specialized software, mobile applications, and high-performance hardware integration. The core philosophy drives toward professionalizing informal or semi-formal markets (such as HVAC-R services) by providing enterprise-grade tools to small and medium businesses (SMBs) and individual professionals.

**Vision:**
To become a leader in the digital transformation of technical trades and specialized education in Brazil and beyond. AC Tech envisions a future where agentic Artificial Intelligence is seamlessly integrated into everyday workflows, effectively "augmenting" human capabilities rather than replacing them. This is evident in their aggressive adoption of "Agentic AI" for their own internal development (TechAI) and user-facing products (TechAir AI).

## 3. The "Agentic" Development Methodology

AC Tech distinguishes itself through a radical internal development philosophy known as the **"Agentic Swarm."** Unlike traditional dev shops that rely solely on human coding capacity, AC Tech has operationalized a fleet of AI agents to handle specific verticals of the development lifecycle. This system, internally branded as **TechAI** or **TechAgents**, allows the two-person team to output code and features at a velocity comparable to much larger organizations.

**Key Components of the Methodology:**
*   **Specialized Personas:** The company employs distinct AI personas for different tasks: `@TechPlan` for architecture, `@TechCode` for implementation, `@TechTest` for quality assurance, and `@TechDesign` for UI/UX.
*   **Cloud-First & Security:** All development adheres to strict security protocols ("Project Rules"), emphasizing that credentials never touch the codebase and that cloud resources remain secure.
*   **Google Antigravity IDE:** The team utilizes the advanced Google Antigravity environment, a specialized IDE designed to maximize the potential of agentic coding, further reinforcing their commitment to cutting-edge tools.

## 4. Product Ecosystem & Market Verticals

AC Tech operates a "Vertical SaaS" model, targeting specific industries with deep, tailored solutions rather than generic tools.

### A. The Flagship: TechAir (HVAC-R Sector)
The company's primary commercial product is **TechAir**, a mobile super-app designed for HVAC (Heating, Ventilation, and Air Conditioning) and Refrigeration professionals.
*   **Market Gap:** The Brazilian HVAC market is characterized by thousands of independent technicians who often struggle with administrative formalization (quotes, PMOC compliance, invoicing).
*   **Solution:** TechAir digitizes the entire workflow, from the initial client visit (AR measurements, digital quotes) to execution (PMOC generation, technical calculations) and post-service management (financial dashboard).
*   **Impact:** It empowers individual technicians to present themselves as structured companies, increasing their competitiveness and operational efficiency.

### B. Education Tech: AC Educacional
AC Tech develops and maintains the digital infrastructure for **AC Educacional**, a premier language learning platform.
*   **The Portal:** Located at `educacional.ac-tech.pro`, this is not a simple website but a comprehensive custom Learning Management System.
*   **Technology:** Built with Astro 4.x using React islands, hosted on Cloudflare Pages, with Firebase backend.
*   **Philosophy:** This project demonstrates AC Tech's ability to deploy complex, enterprise-grade educational infrastructure, moving beyond simple app development into full-stack platform management.

### C. Internal Tooling: TechAI & Microsoft Ultimate Installer
The company commercializes or open-sources its own internal efficiency tools:
*   **TechAI:** An ecosystem of AI agents that automates software development, now being packaged as an IDE extension for broader use. Source: `D:\TechAI`
*   **Microsoft Ultimate Installer:** A powerful automation script developed to streamline the setup of Windows environments. Originally an internal tool to set up the founders' machines quickly, it evolved into a sophisticated product capable of debloating Windows, installing Office 365, and configuring system drivers automatically. Source: `D:\Microsoft Ultimate Installer`
